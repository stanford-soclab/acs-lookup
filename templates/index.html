American Community Survey

The American Community Survey (ACS) is an ongoing statistical survey by the U.S. Census Bureau that gathers information on variables such as jobs and occupations, educational attainment, and income, 27 of which are available for selection on this website.

The dataset provided is the ACS 5-Year Data for the years 2009-2013, and is organized by county. For finer geographical granularity, the dataset has been further organized by zipcode.

For more information on the American Community Survey or on accessing datasets online, see the link below.
